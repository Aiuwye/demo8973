Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

How does the free trial work? +

Our free trial gives you full access to the Professional plan for 14 days. No credit card is required to start, and you can upgrade or cancel at any time. If you decide to subscribe, your data and dashboards will be preserved.

Can I change plans or cancel my subscription? +

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade or cancel your subscription at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades or cancellations will be applied at the end of your current billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. For Business and Enterprise plans, we also offer invoice payment options. Contact our sales team for more information.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions? +

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified non-profit organizations, educational institutions, and startups. Contact our sales team for more information on eligibility and discounts.

What happens if I exceed my data storage limit? +

If you approach your data storage limit, we'll notify you so you can either upgrade to a higher tier or optimize your data usage. We don't automatically charge for overages, but consistent overages will require an upgrade to ensure optimal performance.
