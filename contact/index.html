Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about WinAxis AI

How long does it take to implement WinAxis AI? +

Implementation time varies based on your specific requirements and data environment. For most organizations, initial setup and configuration can be completed within 2-4 weeks, with full implementation typically taking 4-8 weeks. Our approach emphasizes rapid deployment and early value delivery, with incremental enhancements over time.

Do I need technical expertise to use WinAxis AI? +

No, WinAxis AI is designed for users of all technical levels. Our natural language interface allows anyone to interact with data through conversational queries, without needing SQL knowledge or technical expertise. Advanced users can still leverage more technical features when needed.

How does WinAxis AI ensure data security? +

We implement comprehensive security measures including end-to-end encryption, role-based access controls, data masking for sensitive information, and detailed audit logs. Our platform complies with major security standards including SOC 2, GDPR, and HIPAA. We also offer deployment options that keep your data within your secure environment.

How is WinAxis AI priced? +

WinAxis AI offers flexible pricing models based on your organization's size, data volume, and specific requirements. Our plans start with a core platform license plus additional modules based on your needs. We also offer consumption-based pricing for organizations with variable usage patterns. Contact our sales team for a custom quote tailored to your situation.

Can WinAxis AI integrate with our existing systems? +

Yes, WinAxis AI is designed for seamless integration with your existing data infrastructure. We offer 40+ pre-built connectors for popular databases, cloud services, and business applications. Our platform can be integrated with both cloud and on-premises systems, and our team can develop custom connectors for proprietary systems if needed.
